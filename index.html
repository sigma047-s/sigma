COMMON PC PERFORMANCE ISSUES

These are the most frequent problems that prevent your PC from reaching its full potential

Faulty Operating System

A misconfigured or corrupted OS can significantly slow down your computer and cause instability during gaming sessions.

Outdated Drivers

Old or incorrect drivers can cause performance issues, crashes, and prevent your hardware from working optimally.

CPU/GPU Underperformance

Improper configuration or thermal throttling can drastically reduce your processor and graphics card capabilities.

Faulty BIOS Firmware

An outdated or misconfigured BIOS can prevent your hardware from functioning at its full potential.

Over-Optimization

Many online "optimizations" actually hurt performance by disabling essential services or breaking system functionality.

Registry Issues

A bloated Windows registry can slow down your system and cause various errors and instability.

Overheating

Poor thermal management leads to throttling, reduced performance, and hardware degradation.

Background Processes

Unnecessary background applications consuming valuable system resources that should be dedicated to gaming.
